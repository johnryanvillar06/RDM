Dear Preslie De Andres,

Hi, you might be wondering why I created this webpage for you. Honestly, I'm not entirely sure myself, but I think it was back on March 30, during our first gaming session together. I put it together while we were playing, and though you might have heard that I was working on a webpage, I felt really shy about sharing it.

It might sound crazy, but during our first game of Mobile Legends, I felt like you were "the one." As we played, there was something in my heart telling me that if you were truly a gift from God, I would want to keep you close. However, when you mentioned that you weren't really interested in me, it felt like my world was collapsing. You don't need to apologize; I understand that I might not be your type. It's just that my heart was telling me to pursue you.

I still remember your voice and often ask myself how your school and day are going, especially today with the weather being so hot.

Thanks for everything.

Sincerely,
John Ryan Villar
